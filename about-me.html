About Me

General information about me and my campaign

Who am I?


Hello internet! I'm Pranav Sharma, the year 11 representative for learning, and I'm running for the positions of School Captain, Leader of Learning, and Leader of Arts for the 2021 school year. This page will keep you updated on my campaign, as well as important events in the coming weeks regarding the election process.

Why am I?

  • I'm running for leadership because I have several (hundred) ideas on how to improve our school's performance across the board. Our facilities and students are, in the words of our principal, "as good as any," but despite this, we fail, year after year, to achieve our potential, both as individuals and as a school. 
  • I've already started implementing some of these ideas, such as my Schoolnotes Portal, and the introduction of the National Young Writers Program, which you'll hear more about in the next few weeks. A leadership position provides me with a better grounding on which I can pitch ideas in the future.

Inexperienced leaders are quick to lead before knowing anything about the people they intend to lead. But mature leaders listen, learn, and then lead.

John C. Maxwell
  • As the current year representative for learning, I have a firm grasp on the workload and expectations which face the year 12 leaders on a daily basis (some of them are even nominees for my campaign!).
